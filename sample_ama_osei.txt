General Knowledge Course Examination

What is photosynthesis?
The natural process where green plants turn sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into nutrients.

Name the largest ocean on Earth.
The Pacific Ocean.

What causes a solar eclipse?
It happens when the moon passes directly between the Earth and the sun, casting a shadow on Earth.

Who wrote the play "Romeo and Juliet"?
Shakespeare.

Why do humans need oxygen?
Our bodies need it to convert glucose into energy at a cellular level, which keeps our organs functioning.

What is the capital city of France?
Paris.

What is the main function of the human heart?
To pump oxygen-rich blood throughout the body's circulatory system.

Define the term "gravity".
The force that pulls things down to the ground.

How many continents are there on Earth?
Five.

What is the primary language spoken in Brazil?
Spanish.

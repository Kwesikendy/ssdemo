General Knowledge Course Examination

What is photosynthesis?
A chemical process used by plants and other organisms to convert light energy into chemical energy (sugars).

Name the largest ocean on Earth.
The Pacific.

What causes a solar eclipse?
A solar eclipse occurs when the moon aligns perfectly between the sun and Earth, temporarily blocking the sunlight.

Who wrote the play "Romeo and Juliet"?
William Shakespeare wrote it.

Why do humans need oxygen?
For cellular respiration, which allows our cells to generate ATP energy to sustain life.

What is the capital city of France?
Paris is the capital.

What is the main function of the human heart?
It acts as a muscular pump that continuously circulates blood, delivering oxygen and nutrients to tissues.

Define the term "gravity".
Gravity is the universal fundamental force of attraction that exists between any two masses or physical bodies in the universe.

How many continents are there on Earth?
There are 7 continents.

What is the primary language spoken in Brazil?
English.
